For solo operators, delivery breaks when it becomes “infinite chat” and “unverifiable results”.

Use a simple system:

## 1) Milestones (don’t ship everything at the end)

- Kickoff: scope + acceptance + timeline (collect upfront payment)
- Checkpoint: demo-able outcome (collect milestone payment)
- Handoff: final delivery + acceptance checklist (collect final payment)

## 2) Weekly cadence (the most reliable pattern)

Every week:
- 1 sync (15–30 min): decisions + alignment
- 1 deliverable: something reviewable (doc/page/report/deploy)

## 3) Scope creep rule

Change requests = re-scope + re-price + re-schedule.
