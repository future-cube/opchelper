The goal is to reduce chaos: templates + milestones + payment workflow.

## The minimal system

1) Lead intake (who / where / one-line need)  
2) Discovery call script (ask the right 10 questions)  
3) Brief (scope + goals + acceptance)  
4) Pricing + terms (milestones + payment schedule)  
5) Weekly delivery cadence (sync + tangible deliverable)  
6) Change requests = re-scope + re-price  
7) Payment reminders (polite but firm)  
8) Review (turn project into SOP)
